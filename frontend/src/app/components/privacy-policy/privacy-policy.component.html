Out of respect for the Meowcoin community, this website does not use any third-party analytics, third-party trackers, or third-party cookies, and we do not share any private user data with third-parties.


TRUSTED FIRST PARTIES ARE ALSO SECURITY HOLES

Out of respect for the Meowcoin community, this website does not use any first-party cookies, except to store your preferred language setting (if any). However, we do use minimal first-party analytics and logging as needed for the operation of this website, as follows:

  • We use basic webserver logging (nginx) for sysadmin purposes, which collects your IP address along with the requests you make. These logs are deleted after 10 days, and we do not share this data with any third-party. To conceal your IP address from our webserver logs, we recommend that you use Tor Browser with our Tor v3 hidden service onion hostname.

TRUST YOUR OWN SELF-HOSTED Meowcoin Space EXPLORER

For maximum privacy, we recommend that you use your own self-hosted instance of The Meowcoin Space Explorer on your own hardware.
